How I work

My design process, from user research and solution generation through to creation and evaluation of high-fidelity visuals and prototypes.

Research

User interviews and contextual inquiry are used to understand the users, their typical activities, motivations and frustrations.

These are analysed and collated into user personas, which are used to help make informed design decisions.

Ideation

Collaborative solution generation starts with rough exploratory sketching with stakeholders & subject matter experts.

This enables an instant discussion to see how the user journey could take shape.

Ideas are cheap, resulting in rapid iteration upon these ideas at a formative stage.

Any gaps or misunderstandings of the requirements are very quickly identified and resolved.

Interaction design

Interaction designs take shape through iterative refinements of lean low fidelity sketches.

A lo-fi approach focuses on the content and interactions, rather than visual details.

Visual design

Beautiful UI designs take shape based on the lo-fi interaction designs.

Layouts are tweaked, typography perfected, everything tailored for the target devices.

Every detail is carefully considered as part of a holistic user experience.

Deliverables

Design deliverables are lean, focusing on communication over documentation.

Consistency is maintained through a component library of reusable UI patterns.

Prototyping

Modern user interfaces are not fixed canvases

High fidelity, fully responsive designs are rapidly prototyped in the browser.

Combining hand-written HTML, CSS & JavaScript with 3rd party solutions such as Bootstrap allows these to be built rapidly.

Allows a true representation of how designs work on all target devices or at any screen size.

Eliminates the need for developers to translate static design files to code, and ensures design integrity is maintained.

Validation

Guerrilla usability testing techniques are used to validate designs.

Heuristic evaluation used as part of a regular design review process to identify issues early.
