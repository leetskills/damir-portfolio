Recent work

Scroll down to see examples of my recent projects that incorporate the full spectrum of UX & UI Design methodologies.

Scoota Rig Campaigns

As the sole product designer working closely with the development team, designed from the ground up Scoota's brand new programmatic distribution and reporting platform.

Pioneered a collaborative design culture that utilised subject matter experts throughout the company and streamlined the ideation phase of the design process.

Utilised the full breadth of the UX design process from initial research, ideation, defining user journeys, interaction design, prototyping and evaluation.

Scoota Rig Style Guide

Atomic design influenced component library and design system

Built a comprehensive web-based atomic design system to maximise consistency throughout the application and increase design efficiency.

This provided a single and definitive source of truth for app designs and components.

Scoota Creative Toolkit

Completely redesigned the creative ad builder toolkit to improve usability prior to launching to external users.

Planned and executed research studies that included gathering requirements from a number of different persona types.

Used a combination of highly interactive HTML and simple Sketch+InVision prototypes based on the need of the development teams for mobile & desktop.

ZONZA Projects

Lead designer on a new production management module to complement the existing core features of ZONZA.

Utilising the full spectrum of user-centred design methodologies, working closely with the Product Management and Development teams, delivered the project successfully.

Responsibilities included setting up a process for the design team, running stakeholder workshops, conducting user research, and managing design output.

ZONZA DAM UI Refresh

Designer and Product Manager tasked with restyling the core DAM pages of ZONZA.

Managing an Agile Scrum team of developers and testers to successfully deliver the project ahead of schedule.

Responsibilities included maintaining a full product backlog, running daily stand-ups, stakeholder reviews and retrospectives, committing/reviewing code and managing QA testing.
